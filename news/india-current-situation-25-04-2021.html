India Current Situation

India has registered 3,49,691 new cases in the last 24 hours. Five states accounted for 53 per cent of the new cases, with Maharashtra alone responsible for 19.21 per cent of the new cases. 2,767 deaths were reported in the country in the last 24 hours. Maximum casualties were reported in Maharashtra (676), followed by Delhi with 357 fatalities. The top five states that have registered the maximum number of Covid-19 cases in the past 24 hours are Maharashtra with 67,160 cases, Uttar Pradesh with 37,944 cases, Karnataka with 29,438 cases, Kerala with 26,685 cases and Delhi with 24,103 cases
