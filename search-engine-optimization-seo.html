Search engine optimization - What is SEO?

SEO refers to tricks and practices that one can apply to a website to increase its traffic, rank, and visibility in a search engine’s results page (SERP). When the website is correctly optimized, there is greater chance of appearing it in top search results of any search engine like Google or Bing. It will ultimately increase the traffic of the website manifold. The optimization process includes different tricks, methods and techniques that are applied to the website itself or other sites that are related to the website content. To know what is SEO, one has to learn the whole process about how the search process works, what is the search page layout, and what are different search engines that one is going to target.

History of SEO

The process of optimizing web content first occurred in 1990, when search engines began indexing websites’ content. At that time It was very simple process than today, where owners submit their sites to search engines. The search engines started and continue to use computer programs called web spiders or crawlers to crawl a website’s pages and index them based on the site’s niche and keywords. In the start SEO was really simple and we just need to add meta tags, which are HTML codes that include information about a website. Meta tags include website's title, description, and keywords which help search engines determine what the website is about and how its content will be indexed.

SEO with Google

With the launch of the Google search engine, the SEO process has become more complicated. This ensures better and more accurate indexing and identifies misuse of search engine optimization tactics such as keyword stuffing, which refers to the inclusion of irrelevant keywords in web pages to mislead search engine crawlers. It was developed. Google then began improving its search technology by adding elements for indexing and ranking websites. For example, off-page optimization factors include external links to your website, and on-page optimization factors relate to the structure and content of your website.

Doing SEO of a website

Correct and standard use of search engines optimization technique known as white Hat SEO helps you place site-links at the top Search engine results. Then this will drive More traffic and higher production to your website Ranking. On the other hand, misuse of search Engine optimization technology may be negative Affects site indexing and prohibits site indexing It will appear in search engine results known as Black Hat SEO.

Importance of SEO

As the amount of content on the web continued to grow, gaining visibility became very competitive, reaching the top of search engine pages and making it easier for search engine users to identify. Therefore, due to this fierce competition, optimizing web content for search engines is a necessary process to ensure good indexing and high ranking. SEO is an important step after building a website and is just as important as designing and developing a website.

SEO Learning

You can find many articles and resources on the web that cover the search engine optimization process. However, these articles alone do not give you a complete understanding of the SEO process. Although the article provides tips and perspectives, we recommend that you use such a structured guide with hands-on practice and experience. This combination will help you better understand the process and learn how to apply different aspects to achieve your search engine optimization goals.

Techniques for SEO

Search engine traffic is one of the biggest contributors to website success, as it can turn this traffic into potential clients or website followers. The SEO process includes several techniques that need to be applied in parallel to move your website to the top of search engine rankings and increase traffic to your website. Search engine optimization techniques include a variety of on-site and off-site strategies known as on-page and off-page optimization techniques. Each type of method is very important and is necessary for a successful SEO.

What is SEO on page

The on-page SEO technology that applies to the website itself makes the website search engine friendly. On-page refers to the optimization method applied to a website or blog page that appears in search engine results when a user enters a particular search term. These methods are the first step in the optimization process when creating a website. Your website design begins as early as when you choose the name and identity of your business or website. On-page SEO techniques include choosing the right domain name for your website. This is the domain name that users enter in their browser to access the website. It also includes creating meta tags to be added to your HTML code, including information about your website such as titles, descriptions, and related keywords, content headings, site content load speed, keywords, site navigation, and site optimization. .. Design structure. The on-page SEO method doesn't drive direct traffic to your website like the off-page SEO method, but it does make search engine crawlers easily accessible to your site content. It helps you categorize and index your site pages more easily and quickly than websites that don't apply these techniques.

What is SEO off page

Unlike on-page optimization techniques, off-page optimization techniques are methods that are applied outside your website to increase rank, traffic, and visibility in search engines. These methods are implemented and directly or indirectly affect website traffic and ranking in search results. With these methods, you can use third-party tools or directories or websites such as social networking websites to build links to websites and improve traffic. Off-page optimization includes link building. This means increasing the number of links that point to the content of your website. Off-page optimization also includes sending your website to search engines and directories. We will promote it through press releases and articles. Add content to SEO on the page and your website. When optimizing your website, it's important to consider both on-page SEO and off-page methods. For example, you can use Facebook's social network page to increase traffic to your website while loading the website quickly or allowing incoming users to leave the website without reading the content.
